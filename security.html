Online Security & Safety

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

Your security is our top priority at Sanrico Mercantile Inc. This guide explains how we protect your data and provides practical tips to help you stay safe while shopping online.

1. Our Security Infrastructure

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

Security Measures:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmission encrypted
  • Secure Data Centers: 24/7 monitored facilities
  • PCI DSS Compliance: Payment security standards
  • Regular Audits: Independent security testing

2. Safe Account Practices

Essential tips for keeping your account secure:

Strong Passwords

• Use 8+ characters with letters, numbers, symbols

• Unique passwords for each account

Two-Factor Authentication

• Enable 2FA for extra security

• Use authenticator apps when possible

Secure Logout

• Log out on shared computers

• Clear browser data after use

3. Safe Shopping Guidelines

Before Shopping: Verify "https://" and padlock icon, check reviews, ensure updated antivirus.

During Shopping: Only enter payment info on secure pages, avoid saving payment details unnecessarily.

After Shopping: Monitor accounts for unauthorized transactions, save receipts and confirmations.

Avoid Scams: Be wary of deals too good to be true, urgent pressure tactics, and suspicious payment requests.

4. Payment Security

Our Protection: We use tokenization (don't store full card details), fraud detection systems, and secure payment processing.

Your Security: Consider using credit cards over debit cards, virtual card numbers, and digital wallets for additional protection.

Device Protection: Keep software updated, use antivirus software, avoid public Wi-Fi for shopping, and use VPNs when necessary.

5. Contact Security Team

Report Issues: If you suspect account compromise or security problems, contact us immediately.

  1. Change your password immediately
  2. Contact our security team
  3. Monitor your financial accounts
  4. Report to authorities if money lost
